3D Dinosaur Museum

This is a website that provides people with knowledge about dinosaurs. People can observe the dinosaurs with detailed 3D models and videos

This project is suitable for children who are just starting to access new knowledge, it will help children easily promote their rich imagination.

This project can be expanded to include more varieties of dinosaurs, or it can be used as the basis for projects about animals, objects, traffic, etc. The potential for growth is very great.
